WHAT PERSONAL INFORMATION DO WE
COLLECT ABOUT YOU?

We collect personal information about you when you create a Maven account, use our Services, request
or participate in an appointment with Maven Practitioners and/or the Care Team, or otherwise interact
with Maven. The information we collect includes the following categories of personal information from or
about you:

  • Identifiers, such as your full name, home and work address, phone number, email address,
    username and password, employee or member identification number, policy or plan number, and
    Internet protocol (“IP“) address.
  • Health information, such as you or your dependents’ wellness background, weight, height, lifestyle,
    medical history, medical condition, genetic information, healthcare providers, your reason(s) for
    visiting a healthcare provider, date of visit(s), images or videos, diagnoses, treatment plans,
    laboratory results, payment data, insurance information, claims data, and information about your
    health and/or disability status, including reproductive health information.
  • Demographic information, such as you or your dependents’ age, date of birth, sex, gender, national
    origin, citizenship, race, marital status, sexual orientation, medical condition, genetic information,
    and information about your health and/or disability status, including reproductive health,
    information.
  • Usage information, such as information about your usage of the Platform or the Services.
  • Device and electronic network activity information, including information from devices you use to
    connect to our Services, such as your mobile device. The information we receive from these
    devices may include browsing IP address, physical location including Global Position System
    (“GPS“) data, unique device identifiers such as Media Access Control (“MAC“) address, usage
    information, crash data, device performance characteristics and hardware information, battery
    status, WiFi network information, browsing history, search history, clickstream patterns, session
    information, browser and operating system type, navigation paths, date/time stamps, cookie
    identifiers, language preferences, profile or user information associated with your social media
    account, and other information about how you interact with our Site.
  • Professional information, such as employer details and contact information, office location, and
    other professional details.
  • Transaction information, such as claims information, information about payment methods, and
    related financial information.
  • Information you submit to us via the Services while communicating with our Care Team or
    Practitioner network.

Some of the information that we collect may be considered sensitive personal information in some
territories, including your login name together with the password that you use to access your user
account on the Services, precise geolocation, racial or ethnic origin, sexual lifestyle and/or health
information, sexual orientation, reproductive health information and health and genetic data.

In general, we process your personal information as a “data controller” or a “business” under applicable
privacy laws, meaning that we determine how to process and protect your personal information. We also
process a small amount of personal information as a “data processor” or “service provider” under
applicable privacy laws, specifically information relating to your eligibility for our Services under an
employer’s benefits plan.
